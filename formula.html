Flow & Area Dynamics

$$ V_e = \sqrt{\frac{2\gamma}{\gamma - 1} \cdot R T_c \cdot \left(1 - \left(\frac{P_e}{P_c}\right)^{\frac{\gamma - 1}{\gamma}}\right)} $$
$$ M_e = \sqrt{\frac{2}{\gamma - 1} \left[ \left(\frac{P_c}{P_e}\right)^{\frac{\gamma - 1}{\gamma}} - 1 \right]} $$
$$ \dot{m} = \frac{F}{V_e} $$
$$ \frac{A_e}{A_t} = \frac{1}{M_e} \left[ \frac{1 + \frac{\gamma - 1}{2} M_e^2}{\frac{\gamma + 1}{2}} \right]^{\frac{\gamma + 1}{2(\gamma - 1)}} $$
$$ A_t = \frac{\dot{m}}{P_c} \cdot \sqrt{\frac{R T_c}{\gamma} \cdot \left(\frac{\gamma + 1}{2}\right)^{\frac{\gamma + 1}{2(\gamma - 1)}}} $$
$$ d_t = \sqrt{\frac{4A_t}{\pi}} $$
$$ d_e = \sqrt{\frac{4A_e}{\pi}} $$

Geometry & Structural Design

$$ d_c = 3 d_t $$
$$ L_{conv} = \frac{d_c - d_t}{2 \tan(\alpha)} $$
$$ L_{div} = \frac{d_e - d_t}{2 \tan(\beta)} $$
$$ L_{total} = L_c + L_{conv} + L_{div} $$
$$ t_w = \frac{P_c \cdot d_c}{16000} $$

Flow Analysis

$$ P_e = P_c \left(1 + \frac{\gamma - 1}{2} M_e^2\right)^{-\frac{\gamma}{\gamma-1}} $$
$$ T_e = T_c \left(1 + \frac{\gamma - 1}{2} M_e^2\right)^{-1} $$
$$ V_e = M_e \sqrt{\gamma R T_e} $$
$$ F = \dot{m} V_e + (P_e - P_a) A_e $$
$$ I_{sp} = \frac{V_e}{g} $$
